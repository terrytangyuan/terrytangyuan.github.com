I am currently a senior software engineer at Ant Group , building AI infrastructure and AutoML platform.

Meanwhile, I've been involved in many numerous popular open-source software, including TensorFlow , XGBoost , Apache MXNet , ElasticDL , and Kubeflow . Please check out my projects page and GitHub page for more information. I am also the co-author of two best-selling books TensorFlow in Practice and Dive into Deep Learning (with TensorFlow) .

Before joining Ant Group, I was a senior AI platform engineer at H2O.ai where I primarily worked on open source machine learning platform H2O and Driverless AI . Prior to H2O.ai, I led a team at Uptake that built our scalable data science platform to monitor the condition of industry assets such as trains, airplanes, and wind turbines to avoid machine failures and reduce downtime. I also led all the open source initiatives within Uptake data science team.

I graduated from the Pennsylvania State University in Mathematics with honors. I was the co-founder of DataNovo where I built the analytics platform for predictive insights, keywordless prior art search, and product assessment on infringement for law firms, patent attorneys, etc.
