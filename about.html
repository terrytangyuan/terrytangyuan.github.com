Yuan is a principal software engineer at Red Hat, working on OpenShift AI. Previously, he has led teams to build AI infrastructure and platforms at various companies, including Alibaba and Akuity . He's a project lead of Argo and Kubeflow , a maintainer of TensorFlow and XGBoost , and an author of many open source projects . In addition, Yuan authored three machine learning books and published numerous impactful papers. He's a regular conference speaker, technical advisor, leader, and mentor at various organizations.
