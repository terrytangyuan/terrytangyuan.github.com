Curriculum Vitae 履历
The more you give, the more you receive.

I am currently a senior software engineer at Ant Financial, building AI infrastructure and AutoML platform.

Meanwhile, I've authored and contributed numerous popular open-source softwares, including TensorFlow, XGBoost, Apache MXNet, ggfortify, and reticulate. Please check out my projects page and Github page for more information. I am also the author of best-selling book TensorFlow in Practice which is the first book teaching TensorFlow in Chinese. It has becoming training material for many companies and research labs in China and has been translated to several other languages such as traditional Chinese and Korean.

Before joining Ant Financial, I was a senior AI platform engineer at H2O.ai where I primarily worked on open source machine learning platform H2O and Driverless AI. Prior to H2O.ai, I led a team at Uptake that built our scalable data science platform to monitor the condition of industry assets such as trains, airplanes, and wind turbines to avoid machine failures and reduce downtime. I also led all the open source initiatives within data science team.

I graduated from the Pennsylvania State University in Mathematics with honors. I was the co-founder of DataNovo where I built the analytics platform for predictive insights, keywordless prior art search, and product assessment on infringement for law firms, patent attorneys, etc.
